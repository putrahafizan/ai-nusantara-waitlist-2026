## 📊 Posting Strategy

### Week 1: Launch

| Platform | Day 1 | Day 2 | Day 3 | Day 4-5 | Day 6-7 |
|----------|-------|-------|-------|---------|---------|
| **LinkedIn** | Initial post | Personal story | Share landing page | Comment engagement | Share feedback |
| **Twitter** | Thread x1 | Tweet x2 | Tweet x2 | Engage replies | Thread x1 |
| **Instagram** | Post + Story | Story (poll) | Reels (demo) | Story (Q&A) | Post (testimonials) |
| **Facebook** | Post groups | Post timeline | Share to academic groups | Engage comments | Follow up posts |
| **WhatsApp** | 3 groups | 3 groups | Follow up DMs | Answer questions | Thank you message |

### Content Mix:
- 40% Educational (tips, research advice)
- 30% Personal (story, behind-the-scenes)
- 20% Promotional (landing page, features)
- 10% Engagement (polls, questions)

### Best Times to Post:
- **LinkedIn:** Tue-Thu, 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM
- **Twitter:** Mon-Fri, 8-10 AM, 5-7 PM
- **Instagram:** Mon-Fri, 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Facebook:** Tue-Thu, 1-4 PM
- **WhatsApp:** Avoid 9-5 work hours

## 🎯 Engagement Tactics

### Respond to Every Comment:
- "Thanks for feedback! What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
- "Great question! Let me explain more about [feature]..."
- "Appreciate the share! Do you know anyone else who'd benefit?"

### Use Polls & Questions:
- "What's your biggest research pain point?"
- "How many hours do you spend on literature review weekly?"
- "Would you pay Rp 200K/month for tools that save 10 hours/week?"

### Share User Feedback:
- "Just interviewed Prof X from [University]. He said [quote]. This validates our thesis!"
- "5 people joined waitlist today! Terima kasih! 🙏"

### Behind-the-Scenes:
- Share screenshots of interviews (anonymous)
- Show development progress (even if small)
- Document your journey building this

## ⚠️ Community Guidelines

### DO:
✅ Be genuine and helpful
✅ Respond to every comment/DM
✅ Share valuable content (not just promo)
✅ Give credit to others
✅ Follow group rules

### DON'T:
❌ Spam the same message repeatedly
❌ Post in irrelevant groups
❌ Ignore negative feedback
❌ Over-promote (80% value, 20% promo)
❌ Buy fake followers/engagement
