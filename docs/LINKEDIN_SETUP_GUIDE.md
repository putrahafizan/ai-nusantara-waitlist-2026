```

**Template B: Soft Approach (Less Salesy)**

```
Hi Pak/Bu [Nama],

Saya [nama], seorang [background Anda] di Indonesia.

Saya sedang melakukan riset tentang produktivitas akademik dan'd love to connect dengan dosen-dosen seperti Pak/Bu.

Terima kasih!
```

**Template C: Mutual Connection (Best Conversion)**

```
Hi Pak/Bu [Nama],

Saya melihat kita both connected dengan [Mutual Connection Name].

[Mutual Connection] mentioned that Pak/Bu expert di [bidang], dan saya sedang membangun tools untuk bantu produktivitas riset akademik.

Would love to connect dan learn lebih banyak tentang work Pak/Bu!

Terima kasih,
[Nama]
```

**Tips:**
- Personalize setiap message (mention specific research mereka)
- Keep it under 300 characters (LinkedIn limit)
- Don't pitch di connection request
- Focus on "I want to learn" bukan "I want to sell"

### Step 3: Acceptance & Rapport Building

**Setelah mereka accept:**

**Day 1 (Acceptance day):**
```
Terima kasih sudah connect, Pak/Bu [Nama]!

Saya sudah lihat beberapa publications Pak/Bu tentang [topic mereka]. Sangat interesting!

Kalau berkenan, saya'd love to learn lebih banyak tentang research challenges yang Pak/Bu hadapi.

**Day 2-3 (Engage):**
- Like & comment 2-3 postingan mereka
- Comment should be thoughtful, bukan "great post!"

**Example good comments:**
```
"Insightful paper, Pak/Bu! Saya especially interested di bagian [specific part]. Apakah ini challenge yang sering Pak/Bu temui di research?"

"Congrats dengan publicationnya! Quick question: untuk literature review untuk paper ini, kira-kira berapa banyak papers yang Pak/Bu baca?"
```

**Day 4-5 (Soft pitch):**
```
Hi Pak/Bu [Nama],

Saya sedang melakukan validation research untuk tools yang bisa bantu literature review jadi 10x lebih cepat.

Kalau Pak/Bu punya 10 menit free sometime this week, I'd love to ask Pak/Bu beberapa questions tentang research workflow Pak/Bu.

No sales pitch, just research. 😊

Let me know kalau Pak/Bu interested!
```

## ⚠️ PART 7: Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ DON'T:

1. **Spam connection requests**
   - Jangan kirim same message ke 100 orang tanpa personalize
   - Jangan pitch di connection request

2. **Over-promote**
   - 80% value, 20% promo
   - Jangan setiap post adalah "join my waitlist"

3. **Ignore engagement**
   - Reply SEMUA comments
   - Thank people untuk support

4. **Be inauthentic**
   - Share real challenges, bukan hanya successes
   - Be vulnerable, not just "crushing it"

5. **Burn bridges**
   - Jangan argue di comments
   - Jkenalan lalu hilang (ghosting)

### ✅ DO:

1. **Personalize everything**
   - Mention specific research mereka
   - Reference their posts

2. **Provide value first**
   - Share insights, tips, learnings
   - Help sebelum asking

3. **Be consistent**
   - Post 2-3x per week
   - Engage daily

4. **Build relationships**
   - Focus di quality connections
   - Help people without expecting anything back

5. **Track metrics**
   - What posts perform best?
   - What DMs get responses?
   - Optimize berdasarkan data
